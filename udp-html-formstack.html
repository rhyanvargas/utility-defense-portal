Why “Utility Defense”?

Utility Defense, by Zeronet, is a free auditing service in which we carefully examine your utility bills for mistakes and discrepancies. Utilities do make frequent billing mistakes which can include incorrect tariff classifications, miscommunication between providers and suppliers, faulty tax rates, and about a dozen other types of errors. We also look at your supply rates and contracts to let you know whether you are paying above-market pricing for your electricity and natural gas. If you don’t have an in-house utility bill expert already working on your behalf, you need Utility Defense.
